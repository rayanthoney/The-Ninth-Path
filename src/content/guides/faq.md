Here are standard answers to recurring support requests.

### Q: Do I have to read the entire guide?
**A**: Yes. This list has many intertwined dependencies. Skipping pages or steps will lead to immediate instability.

### Q: The game crashes to desktop (CTD) on launch. What did I miss?
**A**: 
1. Re-run LOOT and verify there are no red errors (missing masters).
2. Ensure you have installed the correct VC++ Redistributable.
3. Make sure **OneDrive is disabled** and your mod directory is excluded from active antivirus scanners.

### Q: Can I add my own graphics mods?
**A**: Adding textures is generally safe as long as they don't contain ESP/ESM plugins. Adding mods with script/plugin contents will void support.
